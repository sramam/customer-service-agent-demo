# F5 Distributed Cloud (XC) — Customer Overview

**Audience:** customers using SaaS-delivered WAAP, load balancing, and multi-cloud connectivity

## What it provides

F5 Distributed Cloud (often referenced as **XC**) delivers security and application networking as a service, including:

- **Web Application Firewall** with managed rule sets and bot defenses.
- **Global load balancing** across regions and cloud providers.
- **Mesh connectivity** between sites and cloud workloads with centralized policy.
- **API protection** and bot mitigation for modern application interfaces.

## Features

- **Multi-cloud and edge:** single control plane for policies across public clouds and on-premises connectors.
- **Observability:** dashboards for requests, threats, latency, and errors; export options for SIEM and analytics tools.
- **Certificate lifecycle:** managed certificates and automation options depending on tier.
- **Role-based access** for teams operating shared tenants.

## Common workflows

- Onboard an application by defining domains, origins, and TLS certificates.
- Attach WAF policies and tune exclusions based on observed false positives.
- Use analytics views to review threats and latency.

## Support options

- **SaaS platform support** includes service availability commitments per your **service description** or **SLA** attachment.
- **Technical support** for configuration, integrations, and incidents is delivered through the standard portal and phone queues; **severity** drives response targets.
- **Premium or enterprise** programs may add **technical account management**, **quarterly reviews**, or **faster escalation** — see your contract.
- **Professional services** for complex migrations, multi-site design, and custom integrations are optional add-ons.

## Billing and entitlements

- Usage may combine **base subscriptions** and **metered** components (for example, bandwidth, requests, or protected applications). Refer to your order form for committed tiers and overage rules.
- **Trials** and **sandboxes** may be available for evaluation; usage limits apply.

## Compliance and data

- Data residency and processing terms are defined in your **agreement** and **DPA**; certain regions may have dedicated options on higher plans.
